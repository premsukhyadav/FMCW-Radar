# Electromagnetic Wave

Radar uses electromagnetic waves traveling at approximately the speed of light:

\[ c \approx 3 \times 10^8\ \text{m/s} \]

## Core quantities

- Frequency \(f\) (Hz)
- Wavelength \(\lambda = c/f\)
- Phase \(\phi\)
- Amplitude

Higher frequencies give shorter wavelengths, enabling finer angular resolution with compact antenna arrays.
